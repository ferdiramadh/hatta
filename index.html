About Us

We grew from Bogor Agricultural University. And, we know what farmers need. Easy to sell commodity with a fair price and fast. So, we build digital commerce to solve farmers selling the product. They don't need to confuse to sell the product, we'll handle all the marketing.

But, to build sustainable farming we have to build and grow together. We bring back the power of co-ops economy, partners with farmers around Indonesia into digital. Grow together are what we believe, not individual move. Farmers strong together not alone.

Hatta is the first Indonesia Vice President name we have chosen. To remember his contribution introducing "Co-ops Economy" in Indonesia.
